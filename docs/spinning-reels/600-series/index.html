600 Series

The 600 series spinning reel is a versatile large spinning reel with equal size and power.

Technical Specifications

Sizes & Capabilities:

  • Weight: 25 oz.
  • Max Drag: 40 lbs.
  • Power Drag Option: 40-50 lbs.
  • Gear Ratio: 4.78
  • Bearings: 9
  • Line Retrieval: 33 inches

Approximate Braided Line Capacity:

  • 400/40 yds/lbs
  • 350/50 yds/lbs
  • 300/60 yds/lbs

Approximate Mono Line Capacity:

  • 210/20 yds/lbs
  • 165/25 yds/lbs
  • 140/30 yds/lbs

Fishing Recommendations:

  • Sailfish
  • Swordfish
  • King Mackerel
  • Mahi Mahi
