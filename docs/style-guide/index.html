Emphasis

Emphasis, aka italics, with asterisks or underscores. *asterisks* or _underscores_

Strong emphasis, aka bold, with asterisks or underscores. **asterisks** or __underscores__

Combined emphasis with asterisks and underscores. **asterisks and _underscores_**

Strikethrough uses two tildes. Scratch this. ~~Scratch this.~~
